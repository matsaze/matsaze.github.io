Likot Kônònul

Summary

Likot Kônònul was a male dwarf born in 199. He was of unknown parentage. He was the Mayor of Othbemlimul, "The Pink Gold" and citizen of Uzol Etost, "The Oil of Containing". He was a worshipper of Ingish Nishvutok.

Description

He was flimsy and clumsy.

He had high cheekbones. He had a scratchy voice. His long beard was braided. His very long sideburns were braided. His long mustache was neatly combed. His hair was clean-shaven. His teeth were crowded. His ears were narrow. His nose was slightly hooked. His hair was dark tan. His skin was copper. His eyes were jade.

He had great analytical abilities, a great kinesthetic sense, an ability to read emotions fairly well, a good spatial sense and a feel for music, but he had a meager ability with social relationships and a little patience.

He never became angry, after having a mandate ignored in 254. He had little time for forgiveness and would generally seek retribution. He was often sad and dejected. He took no pleasure in his talents and appearance. He was somewhat quarrelsome, though he was conflicted by this for more than one reason. He was often nervous. He did not easily hate or develop negative feelings, after having to conduct an official meeting in a dining room in 252. He tended to assume the worst of two outcomes would be the one that came to pass. He tended not to be swayed by emotional appeals. He occasionally overindulged, and he was troubled by this because he valued self-control. He preferred that everyone lived as harmoniously as possible. He tended to be a bit stubborn in changing his mind about things. He was trusting.

When he was nervous, he tended to stretch his body. He bit his nails when he got bored.

He needed alcohol to get through the working day. He did not mind being outdoors, at least for a time. He didn't really care about anything anymore.

Like others in his culture, he held craftsdwarfship to be of the highest ideals and celebrated talented artisans and their masterworks, greatly prized loyalty, valued family greatly, saw friendship as one of the finer things in life, believed that honesty was a high ideal, greatly respected artists and their works, really respected those that took the time to master a skill, deeply respected those that worked hard at their labors, respected fair-dealing and fair-play, valued cooperation, found merrymaking and partying worthwhile activities, valued martial prowess, valued leisure time and found nature somewhat disturbing. He personally found sacrifice to be one of the highest ideals (due to being caught in the rain in 252), was an absolute believer in the rule of law (a strengthening after having to conduct an official meeting in a dining room in 252), greatly respected those that observed decorum and maintained their dignity (due to wearing old clothing in 256), valued a harmonious existence (due to seeing the goblin Suque Ismanpim's dead body in 252), valued self-control (due to having a mandate ignored in 254), found the pursuit of knowledge to be a waste of effort and didn't particularly respect commerce. He dreamt of raising a family.

He liked pitchblende, steel, black zircon, the color emerald, battle axes, shields, buckets, llamas for their long necks, giant ticks for their ability to expand and the sound of the Rhyming Bewilderment. When possible, he preferred to consume giant cave toad, bat ray, cherry wine and hard wheat flour. He absolutely detests rats.

Chronology

15th of Malachite, 252, Likot Kônònul settled in Amudtun, "Thunderdoors".

19th of Limestone, 252, he became the Mayor of Othbemlimul.

6th of Hematite, 254, he became a tavern keeper at Ozorimush, "The Subtle Dike" in Amudtun.
