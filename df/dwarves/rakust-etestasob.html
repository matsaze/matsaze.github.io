Rakust Etestasob

Summary

Rakust Etestasob was a male dwarf born in 196 (d. 257). He was of unknown parentage. He was the Militia Commander of Othbemlimul, "The Pink Gold", from 250 until his death in 257. He was a renowned metalcrafter from the Kingdom of Uzol Etost. He was a worshipper of Nekol Vesh Ngotol.

Description

He was unbelievably strong, absolutely inexhaustible, amazingly agile and basically unbreakable.

His hair was straight. His long beard was arranged in double braids. His very long mustache was neatly combed. His very long hair was tied in a pony tail. He had a very broad chin. He had a scratchy voice. His somewhat splayed out, narrow ears were very short. His nose was slightly hooked. His hair was gold. His skin was pale pink. His eyes were emerald. His eyes were somewhat narrow. His nose bridge was slightly convex.

He had a stunning feel for spatial relationships, unbreakable focus, uncanny intuition, an unbreakable will, a great kinesthetic sense, a great deal of patience and a good memory, but he had little natural inclination toward music.

He always acted with mercy and compassion at the forefront of his considerations, a strengthening after seeing the goblin Suque Ismanpim die in 252, and he was conflicted by this since he saw these tendencies as an impediment to the quest for power. He sought out exciting and adventurous situations. He tended to be a bit stubborn in changing his mind about things. He tended to form only tenuous emotional bonds with others. He rarely felt discouraged. He took offered help and gifts without feeling particularly grateful. He had a greedy streak. He tended to be swayed by the emotions of others. He had a sense of duty. He had an active imagination. He tended to assume the worst of two outcomes would be the one that came to pass. He could easily fall in love or develop positive sentiments.

He inhaled sharply when he was angry. He spoke very deliberately when he was angry. He often greeted others with a hug.

He needed alcohol to get through the working day. He did not mind being outdoors, at least for a time. He didn't really care about anything anymore.

Like others in his culture, he held craftsdwarfship to be of the highest ideals and celebrated talented artisans and their masterworks, had a great deal of respect for the law, greatly prized loyalty, valued family greatly, saw friendship as one of the finer things in life, believed that honesty was a high ideal, greatly respected artists and their works, really respected those that took the time to master a skill, deeply respected those that worked hard at their labors, valued cooperation, found merrymaking and partying worthwhile activities, valued martial prowess, valued leisure time, respected commerce, valued knowledge and found nature somewhat disturbing. He personally believed that the acquisition of power over others is the ideal goal in life and worthy of the highest respect (due to experiencing trauma in 254) and found the idea of fair-dealing foolish and cheated when he found it profitable. He dreamt of raising a family.

He liked limonite, lead, rock crystal, basking shark leather, sea lamprey tooth, anchovy bone, the color pale pink, maces, scepters, alpacas for their long necks, nightmares for their unfathomably horrifying nature, white millet plants for their grain, the words of The Festive Wisdom and the sound of The Plays of Aquamarine. When possible, he preferred to consume giant kingsnake, banana beer, llama's milk and paradise nut fruits. He absolutely detested purring maggots.

Chronology

25th of Hematite, 250, Rakust Etestasob settled in Amudtun, "Thunderdoors".

9th of Moonstone, 250, he was appointed Militia Commander of Othbemlimul.

In the early autumn of 253, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the early summer of 254, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

16th of Hematite, 254, Kåtdirast Rotik Astesh, "Glimmersabre the Nettle of Cudgels", received its name in Amudtun from him.

Kåtdirast Rotik Astesh was a superior quality iron shield.

24th of Malachite, 254, the Kingdom of Uzol Etost attacked Atu Ngûlo, "The Flies of Inking" of the goblin civilization of Smunstu Nûng, "The Lies of Clinching" at Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice". He led the attack, and the defenders were led by the goblin Amxu Olngöutôst. He outmatched Amxu Olngöutôst with a cunning plan, and the attackers had a strong positional advantage in Âtxuspgas. Dalkammedtob, "The Lean Blockade", led by him, clashed with four goblins in Âtxuspgas, slaying them. The goblin Ror Rekuuslôx attacked him, but he struck down the goblin. For further information, check the report of The Raze of Âtxuspgas of 254.

10th of Galena, 254, Azinison, "Watchplanned", received its name in Amudtun from him.

Azinison was copper battle axe. It was spattered with frozen goblin blood.

In the early winter of 254, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the early summer of 255, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the early winter of 255, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

20th of Moonstone, 255, the Kingdom of Uzol Etost attacked Atu Ngûlo of the goblin civilization of Smunstu Nûng at Âtxuspgas. He led the attack, and the defenders were led by the goblin Osod Titthalalmôsh. He outmatched Osod Titthalalmôsh with a cunning plan, and the attackers had a strong positional advantage in Âtxuspgas. For further information, check the report of The Raze of Âtxuspgas of 255.

26th of Granite, 256, the Kingdom of Uzol Etost attacked Atu Ngûlo of the goblin civilization of Smunstu Nûng at Âtxuspgas. He led the attack, but he had no success in rooting out the settlement. For further information, check the report of The Raze of Âtxuspgas of 256.

In the early summer of 256, he fought in defense of Amudtun. He struck down the goblin Båx Ngonosuksos during the siege.

In the midsummer of 256, the forces of Othbemlimul, led by him, looted the abandoned monastery of Edëmasob, "Keyboards".

In the early winter of 256, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the early summer of 257, he fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the late autumn of 257, he became a member of Kilruddomas, "The Bronze Guild", a metalsmith guild.

6th of Moonstone, 257, Smunstu Nûng attacked Othbemlimul of Uzol Etost at Amudtun. The goblin Getak Adothemat led the attack. Rakust Etestasob was mortally wounded by the goblin Utes Nakonozut during battle and suffocated.

In the early winter of 257, he was buried and memorialized in Amudtun. His memorial slab reads:

"In memory of Rakust Etestasob
Born 196
Suffocated, slain by the goblin Utes Nakonozut in Utu Sox, "The Onslaught of Whips" in the year 257
Militia Commander of The Pink Gold, 250 to 257
Slayer of the Goblin Ror Rekuuslôx
At one with anchovy bone"
