Thîkut Egdothkeskal

Summary

Thîkut Egdothkeskal was a male dwarf born in 165. He was of unknown parentage. He was the Duke of Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice", in the Kingdom of Uzol Etost, "The Oil of Containing". He was the expedition leader of Othbemlimul, "The Pink Gold" and founder of Amudtun, "Thunderdoors". He was a worshipper of Rozsed Nótonkurol När.

Description

He was very slow to tire, quite quick to heal, tough and agile.

His medium-length beard was braided. His very long sideburns were braided. His medium-length mustache was arranged in double braids. His very long hair was tied in a pony tail. His teeth were crowded. He had a scratchy voice. His nose was quite long. His head was somewhat short. His cinnamon skin was wrinkled. His ears had small lobes. His hair was amber with a touch of gray. His eyes were emerald.

He had an iron will and very good intuition, but he had an iffy sense for music, poor creativity and really poor focus.

He was ruled by irresistible cravings and urges. He never deliberated before acting, to the point of being considered thoughtless. He was very quick to anger. He tended to be swayed by the emotions of others. He had an active sense of humor. He tended to be a bit stubborn in changing his mind about things. He wasn't given to flights of fancy. He generally acted with a narrow focus on the current activity. He was trusting.

When he was nervous, he often interrupted others during conversations.

He needed alcohol to get through the working day. He liked working outdoors and grumbled only mildly at inclement weather. He didn't really care about anything anymore.

Like others in his culture, he held craftsdwarfship to be of the highest ideals and celebrated talented artisans and their masterworks, had a great deal of respect for the law, greatly prized loyalty, valued family greatly, saw friendship as one of the finer things in life, believed that honesty was a high ideal, greatly respected artists and their works, really respected those that took their time to master a skill, deeply respected those that worked hard at their labors, respected fair-dealing and fair-play, valued cooperation, valued martial prowess, valued leisure time, respected commerce, valued knowledge and found nature somewhat disturbing. He personally truly valued merrymaking and parties and respected perseverance (due to not having a tomb after gaining another year in 252). He dreamt of raising a family.

He liked bituminous coal, nickel silver, chrysoberyl, bitter orange wood wood, oyster shell, the color crimson, gems, figurines, yaks for their shaggy hair, snail men for their shells, the words of The Playful Bird and the sight of the Sparkle of Cherishing. When possible, he preferred to consume kea, nautilus and pomegranate wine. He absolutely detested cave spiders.

Chronology

15th of Granite, 250, Thîkut Egdothkeskal and companions founded Amudtun.

In the late spring of 250, he became manager of Othbemlimul.

19th of Limestone, 252, he was bestowed the title of Baron of Amudtun by King Kogan Bimilral of Uzol Etost.

16th of Limestone, 253, he was bestowed the title of Count of Amudtun by King Kogan Bimilral of Uzol Etost.

16th of Limestone, 253, he was bestowed the title of Duke of Amudtun by King Kogan Bimilral of Uzol Etost.

10th of Moonstone, 256, he became Duke of Âtxuspgas after the militia-dwarves of Othbemlimul occupied Âtxuspgas.
