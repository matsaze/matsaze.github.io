Èrith Eralmeng

Summary

Èrith Eralmeng Kilrudalis Tôsed was a female dwarf born in 176. She was of unknown parentage. She was the Champion and Militia Commander of Othbemlimul, "The Pink Gold", as well as the former Chief Medical Dwarf. She was a worshipper of Kodor Shinimmast.

Description

She was absolutely inexhaustible, unbelievably strong, incredibly tough and almost never sick.

Her hair was straight. Her very long hair was tied in a pony tail. She had a grating, raspy voice. Her amethyst eyes were close-set. Her ears were very short. Her head was somewhat short. Her nose was slightly hooked. Her dark peach skin was slightly wrinkled. Her hair was auburn with a touch of gray.

She had unbreakable focus, uncanny intuition, an astounding feel for the position of her own body, a stunning feel for spatial relationships, a great feel for social relationships, an iron will, a deep well of patience and a way with words, but she had very bad analytical abilities and a poor memory.

She was in a constant state of internal rage. She was often sad and dejected, after experiencing trauma in 252. She occasionally overindulged. She was rarely happy or enthusiastic, and she was conflicted by this as she valued parties and merrymaking in the abstract. She generally acted impartially and was rarely moved to mercy, and she was disturbed by this as someone who disliked those that sought to acquire power over others. She wasn't particularly ambitious. She was not particularly interested in what others thought of her. She tended to be a little wasteful when working on projects. She liked a little excitement now and then. She liked to brawl.

She often greeted others with a hug.

She needed alcohol to get through the working day. She did not mind being outdoors, at least for a time. She didn't really care about anything anymore.

Like others in her culture, she held craftsdwarfship to be of the highest ideals and celebrated talented artisans and their masterworks, greatly prized loyalty, valued family greatly, saw friendship as one of the finer things in life, believed that honesty was a high ideal, greatly respected artists and their works, really respected those that took their time to master a skill, deeply respected those that worked hard at their labors, respected fair-dealing and fair-play, valued cooperation, found merrymaking and partying worthwhile activities, valued martial prowess, valued leisure time, respected commerce, valued knowledge and found nature somewhat disturbing. She personally would have had the world operate in complete harmony without the least bit of strife or disorder (due to experiencing trauma in 256), hated those who wielded power over others (due to experiencing trauma in 252), valued sacrifice (due to experiencing trauma in 254), found maintaining decorum a silly, fumbling waste of time (due to being caught in a snow storm in 250), did not respect the law and found those that deny their impulses somewhat stiff. She dreamt of crafting a great work of art.

She liked tin glaze, gold, fire agate, giant snail shell, the color pale pink, maces, bucklers and brown recluse spiders for their venomous bite. When possible, she preferred to consume cave lobster, cave fish and date wine. She absolutely detested toads.

Chronology

9th of Sandstone, 250, Èrith Eralmeng settled in Amudtun. On that day, she became the Chief Medical Dwarf of Othbemlimul.

9th of Moonstone, 250, she was assigned to the squad of Dalkammedtob, "The Lean Blockade", under the command of Rakust Etestasob.

In the midautumn of 252, she mortally wounded the goblin Suque Ismanpim, who bled to death, with an iron short sword in Amudtun.

In the early autumn of 253, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the midwinter of 253, she ceased to be the Chief Medical Dwarf of Othbemlimul.

In the early summer of 254, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

24th of Malachite, 254, she fought in an assault against Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice". For further information, check the report of The Raze of Âtxuspgas of 254.

In the early winter of 254, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the midspring of 255, Astelbecor, "Alleytempted" received its name in Amudtun from her.

Astelbecor was a superior quality iron shield.

In the early summer of 255, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the midsummer of 255, Tulonbal Kosoth Tîrdug, "Roadtones the Palace of Brutalizing" received its name in Amudtun from her.

Tulonbal Kosoth Tîrdug was a well-crafted steel short sword. It was spattered with goblin blood.

In the early winter of 255, she fought in defense of Amudtun. During the siege, she struck down the goblin Corud Molucaspa with Tulonbal Kosoth Tîrdug.

20th of Moonstone, 255, she fought in an assault against Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice". For further information, check the report of The Raze of Âtxuspgas of 255.

In the midwinter of 255, she became a member of Kilruddomas, "The Bronze Guild", a metalsmith guild.

26th of Granite, 256, she fought in an assault against Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice". For further information, check the report of The Raze of Âtxuspgas of 256.

In the early summer of 256, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the midsummer of 256, led by Rakust Etestasob, she looted the abandoned monastery of Edëmasob, "Keyboards".

In the early winter of 256, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the early summer of 257, she fought in defense of Amudtun.

In the early winter of 257, she fought in defense of Amudtun. During the siege, she mortally wounded the goblin Utes Songrulasm, who bled to death, and struck down the goblins Utes Nakonozut and Ngebzo Ozudumâ with Tulonbal Kosoth Tîrdug. Following the death of Rakust Etestasob, she became the Militia Commander of Othbemlimul. At that time, she also became the Champion of Othbemlimul.
