Amudtun, "Thunderdoors"

By 250, all that was left of the dwarven civilization of Uzol Etost was the dark dwarven fortress of Ngebosmunstu, "Worklie", home of King Kogan Bimilral. It was situated east of the dark goblin pit of Aspouksos, "Lootstole", in The South Murk.

In response to the encroaching goblins from the southeast, a small expedition of 7 dwarves was sent northeast, towards The Admired Point, in the hopes of establishing a prosperous outpost to serve as the kingdom's new capital. On the 15th of Granite, 250, Amudtun, was founded. The expeditioners were:

  • Udib Daroszon the Miner;
  • Zuntîr Dumatmosus the Woodworker;
  • Edëm Mûthkategen the Mason;
  • Tekkud Kivshzim the Metalcrafter;
  • Sigun Regnil the Fisherdwarf;
  • Nil Edëmthosbut the Planter;
  • Thîkut Egdothkeskal the Expedition Leader.

By 10th Felsite, 250, the living quarters had been constructed, and the industrial section had been dug out. The following dwarves were appointed for the following administrative roles: Thîkut Egdothkeskal, as manager; Udib Daroszon, as broker and Nil Edëmthosbut, as bookkeeper.

On the 25th of Hematite, 250, the first wave of migrants arrived in Amudtun. Listed among those migrants were:

  • Nil Mâtzangudib the Woodworker;
  • Urdim ìtdùnfath the Carpenter;

  • Rakust Etestasob the Metalcrafter.

The Outpost Liaison Datan Limuldèg, from the Kingdom of Uzol Etost, arrived on the 11th of Limestone, 250. Bags, thread, cloth, plump helmet and dimple cup spawns; pig tail, cave wheat and sweet pod seeds; rock nuts, crossbows, picks, drinks and plump helmets were requested. In exchange, the liaison requested fish imports.

Èrith Eralmeng was named chief medical dwarf on account of her medical knowledge.

The outpost of Amudtun finished construction of a tavern around the 12th of Timber, 250. It was named Ozorimush, "The Subtle Dike". At around the same time, a temple was built, called Ivireabama, "The Dispersed Blame", to no deity in particular.

On the 9th of Moonstone, 250, Rakust Etestasob was appointed Militia Commander of Othbemlimul, as preparations begun for the establishment of a militia. On the 18th of Moonstone, a squad of 4 dwarves was formed. It went by the name of Dalkammedtob, "The Lean Blockade", and was composed of Rakust Etestasob, Èrith Eralmeng, Lolor Kinemavuz and Zefon Ritanùshrir.

A hospital was built on the 3rd of Granite, 251. It was called Ralbom, "The Silvery Homes". Èrith Eralmeng, the chief medical dwarf, was put in charge of the facility.

During early winter, around the 10th of Moonstone, 251, the farming situation was addressed. A large area, close by the tavern, was dug out and flooded using a spiral ramp that led to the brook. Once the area dried up, farm plots were designated for planting plump helmets, cave wheat, sweet pods and pig tail.

On the 16th of Opal, 251, Lòr Emuthmörul the Herbalist was found missing. He was memorialized on the 5th of Opal, 252, after he was declared dead. His body was found on top of a tree, to the southwest of the fortress' entrance, on the 24th of Galena, 253. It was determined that the cause of death was dehydration.

Åblel Reglerom became a performer for Ozorimush on the 12th of Obsidian, 251.

A clothing industry was established on the 17th of Granite, 252, close by the farming district. Rakust Etestasob the Militia Commander was the only capable clothier in Amudtun at the time.

Thilségked was a set of claystone mechanisms of the highest craftsdwarfship. It was encrusted with rectangular picture jasper cabochons and encircled with bands of alpaca wool, cushion rock salt cabochons and black-cap. They were adorned with hanging rings of picture jasper. The ranks of Dalkammedtob were filled with four more members: Likot Dakonerib the Pump Operator; Reg Inguldeduk the Milker; Likot Taronedëm the Bowyer and Doren Ösirnish the Milker.
