The Raze of Âtxuspgas, 255

Mission Report:

In the early winter of 255, Uzol Etost, "The Oil of Containing", attacked Atu Ngûlo, "The Flies of Inking", of Smunstu Nûng, "The Lies of Clinching", at Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice". The dwarf Rakust Etestasob led the attack, and the defenders were led by the goblin Osod Titthalalmôsh.

In the early winter of 255, the dwarf Rakust Etestasob outmatched the goblin Osod Titthalalmôsh with a cunning plan, and the attackers had a strong positional advantage in Âtxuspgas.

In the early winter of 255, the goblin Anur Kidetolil confronted the dwarf Lolor Kinemavuz.

In the early winter of 255, the goblin Anur Kidetolil was struck down by the dwarf Lolor Kinemavuz in Âtxuspgas.

In the early winter of 255, the goblin Osod Titthalalmôsh attacked the dwarf Likot Taronedëm.

In the early winter of 255, the goblin Osod Titthalalmôsh was struck down by the dwarf Likot Taronedëm in Âtxuspgas.

In the early winter of 255, Othbemlimul, "The Pink Gold", looted treasure from Âtxuspgas.

In the early winter of 255, the forces of Othbemlimul rampaged throughout Âtxuspgas.
