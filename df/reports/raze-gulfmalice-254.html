The Raze of Âtxuspgas, 254

Mission Report:

In the midsummer of 254, Uzol Etost, "The Oil of Containing", attacked Atu Ngûlo, "The Flies of Inking", of Smunstu Nûng, "The Lies of Clinching", at Âtxuspgas, "Gulfmalice". The dwarf Rakust Etestasob led the attack, and the defenders were led by the goblin Amxu Olngöutôst.

In the midsummer of 254, the dwarf Rakust Etestasob outmatched the goblin Amxu Olngöutôst with a cunning plan, and the attackers had a strong positional advantage in Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, Dalkammedtob, "The Lean Blockade", led by the dwarf Rakust Etestasob, clashed with four goblins in Âtxuspgas, slaying them.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Song Zumzustutes attacked the dwarf Likot Taronedëm.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Song Zumzustutes's upper lip was smashed by the dwarf Likot Taronedëm.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Song Zumzustutes was struck down by the dwarf Likot Taronedëm in Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Ror Rekuuslôx attacked the dwarf Rakust Etestasob.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Ror Rekuuslôx was struck down by the dwarf Rakust Etestasob in Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Stozu Xuspgassagus confronted the dwarf Sigun Regnil.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Stozu Xuspgassagus's right ear was smashed by the dwarf Sigun Regnil.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Stozu Xuspgassagus was struck down by the dwarf Sigun Regnil in Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Amxu Olngöutôst attacked the dwarf Urvad Alåthbecor.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Amxu Olngöutôst was struck down by the dwarf Urvad Alåthbecor in Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, Othbemlimul, "The Pink Gold", looted treasure from Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, the forces of Othbemlimul rampaged throughout Âtxuspgas.

In the midsummer of 254, the goblin Era Icalaceta was struck down by the dwarf Sigun Regnil in Âtxuspgas.
